A lead-capture front door you can drop anywhere: a QR code at an event, a social bio link, or embedded on a site. It centralizes collection and automates the follow-up.

## What it does

- **Smart validation:** real-time checks so phone numbers and emails come in clean.
- **Instant alerts:** immediate SMS and email to stakeholders for zero-latency response.
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics and Meta Pixel to track where conversions come from.
- **Mobile first:** built for where most quick lead capture actually happens.

## Data

Everything transmits over encrypted SSL/TLS straight to your endpoint and flows into n8n and the CRM. No data hoarding.
