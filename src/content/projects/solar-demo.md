A complete sales environment built to show what an end-to-end solution looks like for one industry. Branded marketing site, CRM, lead-capture front door, a sales dashboard, and AI agents that handle intake and nurture, wired together the way a real installer would run it.

## What makes it interesting

- **A whole vertical, not a feature.** Branding, CRM, lead flow, dashboard, and agents as one working system, down to the invoices and press releases.
- **An agent team, not one bot.** An orchestrator routes each inbound message to the right specialist: sales, support, or education. A background watcher fires an alert the moment a new lead lands.
- **A repeatable pattern.** The same stack reskins for any service business.
