A complete sales environment built to show what an end-to-end solution looks like for one industry. Branded CRM, a lead-capture front door, and AI agents that handle intake and nurture, wired together the way a real installer would run it.

## What makes it interesting

- **A whole vertical, not a feature.** Branding, CRM, lead flow, and agents as one working system.
- **Agent-driven.** Lead and follow-up agents do the repetitive sales motion.
- **A repeatable pattern.** The same stack reskins for any service business.
